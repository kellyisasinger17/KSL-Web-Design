About Kelly

Every business, artist, and creative professional has story worth telling. Whether you are launching creative business, teaching your craft, or growing small business you have poured your heart into, your website should reflect same care.

Before moving into web development, I trained as classical opera singer. That background taught me what it means to create something deeply personal and share it with world. My experience in education, client-facing work, and relationship building continues to shape every project. I design by listening first, then build each site with creativity, care, and long-term purpose.

Why Cédille?

Cédille comes from French word for cedilla, the small mark beneath Ç in my last name: Lançon.

The cedilla reflects the importance of my husband's French heritage. I also speak French, and French culture has very much shaped our lives and family values. Cédille became fitting symbol for my studio: distinctive, thoughtful, and rooted in heritage.
