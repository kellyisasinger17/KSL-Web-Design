About Kelly

Good design is timeless.

Every business, artist, and creative professional has a story worth telling, and I enjoy discovering what makes each one unique before bringing that story to life.

Before transitioning into web development, I trained as a classical opera singer. As a former musician, I understand what it feels like to create and craft something deeply personal and share it with the world. Whether you're launching a creative business, teaching your craft, or growing a small business you've poured your heart into, your website should reflect the same care you've invested in your work. That perspective continues to shape how I approach every project: with creativity, thoughtful craftsmanship, and the belief that meaningful communication begins by listening first.

My background in education, client-facing roles, and relationship building has taught me that the best design starts with understanding people. Every website is thoughtfully designed, carefully developed, and built to serve your work for years to come.

Based in New York

Proudly serving small businesses and nonprofits in the city and beyond.
