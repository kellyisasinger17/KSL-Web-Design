Recent Commissions

Every project begins with understanding.

A website is more than a collection of pages: it's often the first introduction to your organization, your mission, and the people behind it.

Before design begins, we take the time to understand not only the goals of the project, but the people, values, and vision that drive it. Our goal remains the same: to create a website that reflects the character of the organization and communicates its purpose with clarity and confidence.

Maison Lançon

Luxury Brand Website & Digital Foundation

Maison Lançon is a custom jewelry design business, just opening their doors. They wanted a dynamic website with a luxurious feel to display their own jewelry pieces, and so consumers can order custom jewelry to be designed as well.

  • Custom design
  • Custom inquiry and contact forms
  • Mobile optimization
  • Technical SEO foundations
  • Luxury-inspired brand implementation

Lightning Auto Service

Website Redesign & Business Growth Strategy

Based in south Jersey / Philadelphia, Lightning Auto Service requested a website redesign from their old Wix setup. We worked with them to improve branding, make their content clearer and easier to search, and added several contact forms for local collision repair shops to easily find them online.

  • Website migration and redesign
  • Custom contact forms for collision repair facilities
  • Improved architecture
  • Technical SEO foundations
