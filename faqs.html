Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers before we begin.

Explore common questions about custom websites, migration, SEO, ongoing stewardship, and the design process.

Why do you specialize in small businesses and nonprofits?

As a small business owner myself, I understand the challenges of building something impactful with limited time and resources. I enjoy partnering with organizations where relationships matter, every decision has purpose, and where a thoughtfully-designed website can make a lasting impact. Whether you're serving customers or your community, my goal is to create a website that reflects the quality of your work and helps people connect with it.

What makes KSL Web Design different from other web designers?

Rather than relying on templates or CMS solutions like Wix, Wordpress, or Shopify, I take time to understand your goals, values, audience, and long-term vision before designing a single page. The result is a website that feels authentic to your organization-not just visually appealing, but purposeful and easy to use.

What types of organizations do you work with?

I primarily work with:

  • Small businesses
  • Nonprofit organizations
  • Professional service providers
  • Consultants
  • Creative businesses
  • Educational organizations
  • Community organizations
  • Faith-based organizations

If your organization values thoughtful design and clear communication, we're likely a good fit.

Do you use Wix, WordPress, Shopify, Squarespace, or other website builders?

No. I build custom websites from the ground up using modern front-end development practices. This gives me greater control over performance, accessibility, design, and long-term maintainability.

If you're currently using one of these platforms, I can help migrate your existing website into a fully custom solution.

Why don't you build websites with AI website builders like Lovable or Rork?

Artificial intelligence is an excellent assistant, but it isn't a replacement for thoughtful design.

I use AI to improve efficiency by refining layouts, catching errors, and cleaning up code, but I don't believe your organization's story, strategy, or user experience should be generated with a single prompt.

A meaningful website comes from listening, asking questions, understanding your audience, and making intentional design decisions. AI can support that process, but it shouldn't replace the human thinking behind it.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Absolutely! Whether your current website needs a visual refresh, better organization, improved performance, or a complete rebuild, I can help preserve what works while creating a more modern, effective online presence.

Can you migrate my website from another platform?

Yes, I offer website migration services for organizations looking to move away from platforms like Wix, WordPress, Squarespace, or other builders into a custom website tailored to their goals.

Will my website work on phones and tablets?

Yes, every website is designed to provide a seamless experience across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

Will my website be optimized for search engines?

Every project includes technical SEO foundations to help search engines understand and index your website properly.

This includes page structure, metadata, performance optimization, semantic HTML, and other best practices that create a strong foundation for long-term visibility.

Can you help with branding?

Absolutely! If your organization already has an established brand, I'll build a website that reflects it consistently.

If you're just getting started-or your visual identity needs refinement-I also offer brand identity refreshes, logo redesigns, typography recommendations, and color palette development.

Do you provide website maintenance after launch?

Yes, I offer ongoing Website Stewardship plans at no additional cost that include legal updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support so your website continues performing well long after launch. For any additional pages or content changes to your website, additional costs may apply.

Will I be able to update my website myself?

Because every website is custom built, update options depend on the features included in your project.

For organizations that regularly publish blogs, news, events, or articles, I can build intuitive editing tools specifically for those sections. For everything else, many clients prefer to use a Website Stewardship plan so updates remain consistent and professionally maintained.

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects take between two and four weeks, depending on the size of the website, project complexity, and how quickly content is provided. A detailed timeline is provided before development begins.

What is your typical process, start to finish?

Every project starts with a discovery conversation to gather as many details as possible. We'll discuss your organization, your audience, your goals, your existing website if applicable, and the challenges you're hoping to solve. Once the concept is clarified, I'll develop wireframes to bring your ideas to life. You can make any revisions to the wireframes prior to the website construction. Once design is approved, your website will be built, and I'll send you updates periodically. Prior to publishing, and even after the site is initially published, you'll be able to submit minor revisions and adjustments. Once the project is finalized, revisions can be submitted within two weeks, after which time an additional service charge may be applied.

If I'm unable to deliver on any service that affects the total functionality of your website, I'll issue a partial refund. Labor charges for initial designs may be incurred and will be invoiced separately, in these cases.

Do you only work with clients in New York?

While I'm based in New York, I work remotely with clients throughout the United States. At this time, I don't offer services outside of the United States due to the variety of privacy and accessibility laws internationally.
